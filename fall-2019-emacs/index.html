## All your modifiers are mine The absolute basic key combos you'll need: * C-f and C-b: go forward and backward one character * M-f and M-b: go forward and backward one word * C-n and C-p: go down and up one line * C-a and C-e: go to the beginning and end of a line * M-> and M-<: go to the beginning and end of the buffer * C-x u: undo * C-SPC: begin/end selecting text
* C-w and M-w: cut and copy selection * C-y: paste text * C-k: kill a line * C-h: open help * C-s and C-r: forward and reverse search * C-x C-f: open a file * C-x C-s: save current buffer * C-x s: save all buffers * C-x C-c: quit
